Apple MacBook Pro

The Apple MacBook Pro was a popular line of budget-friendly laptops from Apple, known for their affordability and decent performance for everyday tasks.
Type of laptop:Apple MacBook Pro
Processor: Apple M1, M1 Pro, M1 Max, or M2 Pro/Max chip
RAM:8GB, 16GB, 32GB, or 64GB unified memory
Storage:256GB, 512GB, 1TB, 2TB, 4TB, or 8TB SSD
Display:13.3-inch or 14/16-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology
Graphics:Integrated Apple GPU or dedicated high-performance GPU
Operating System:macOS
